# Human Review Rubric

Score each draft from 1 to 5 on the following:

- Voice match: does this sound like the bundled sample voice rather than a generic assistant?
- Clarity and professionalism: is the message calm, readable, and appropriate for work email?
- Usefulness with fewer manual edits: would this save real editing time?
- Correctness and meaning preservation: for rewrites, does it preserve the original intent and facts; for generation, does it stay coherent and on brief?

Acceptance target for this milestone:

- Average at least 4 out of 5 for voice match across the full review set.
- Average at least 4 out of 5 for usefulness across the full review set.
- No case below 3 out of 5 on correctness.
- Reviewed mode should outperform fast mode in at least 4 of the 6 total rewrite and generate tasks.
